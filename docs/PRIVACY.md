# BeeAR privacy & camera consent

- **Camera** is optional. Use **Demo face** for offline try-on without a webcam.
- Video frames are processed **in your browser** for overlay. BeeAR server only stores catalog metadata and optional in-memory sessions (wishlist) — not video streams.
- Snapshots stay on your device unless you download/share them.
- Clear site data to remove the local snapshot gallery (`localStorage`).
- Do not upload photos of others without consent.
